The thesis

Most edtech AI tries to replace the teacher. We built the opposite.

Sidekick amplifies the human in the room. It knows the curriculum your student is using in class. It spots the prerequisite skills they might have missed.

It suggests the next problem, the next question, the next move. And it remembers everything — so the next session picks up where the last one left off.

Sidekick Briefing · before the session

We watch every session. You get a one-minute briefing.

When a tutor opens the video portal, Sidekick meets them with a personalized briefing — built by analyzing the recording of their last session: the student's work, the dialogue, and the tutor's own teaching moves.

Where the student is

Concepts covered last session, what's mastered, what's still developing — distilled from the video itself, not a separate test.

How the tutor is growing

A specific teaching technique to try today, grounded in what actually happened last session — with an example script and benchmarks against other Step Up tutors.

One action for today

No sprawling report. One focus technique, one sentence they could try, one concrete move to make this session better than the last.

Personalized

Personalized to each student, every time.

Sidekick builds a living profile of each student over time: interests, confidence level, known misconceptions, mastered skills. That profile shapes the examples it surfaces and the scaffolding it recommends. A student who loves basketball gets fraction problems about free-throw percentages. A quieter student gets gentler, more patient prompts.
